Lexie Xu

An Indonesian thriller novelist. She is a Sherlockian, a fan of J.J. Abrams, and fanatic of number 47. Her muse is F4/JVKV. Love Big Bang and Running Man. Live in Bandung with her only son, Alexis Maxwell. Her famous is Johan Series, Omen Series, Dark Series, and JanganDiklik Series.

Johan Series

Consists of 4 books including Obsesi, Pengurus MOS Harus Mati, Permainan Maut, and Teror

Omen Series

Consists of 7 books including Omen, Tujuh Luksisan Horor, Misteri Organisasi Rahasia The Judges, Malam Karnaval Berdarah, Kutukan Hantu Opera, Sang Pengkhianat, dan Target Terakhir

Dark Series

Consists of 6 books including Rahasia Tergelap, Pemburuan dalam Kegelapan, Malaikat Berhati Gelap, Dibalik Sosok Gelap, Buku Harian Pangeran Kegelapan, and Akhir Tergelap

JanganDiklik Series

Currently consists of 3 books including Rahasia Ayu, Ketika Sukma Terjaga, and Surat Untuk Hera
